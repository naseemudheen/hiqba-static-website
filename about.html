ABOUT HIQBA TOURISM

We believe that life's greatest lessons are learned through travel. Life doesn’t happen when you color inside the lines. It’s only when break free, trust your impulse and intentionally live with your eyes wide open that you become the person you're meant to be. This is what it means to live life with no regrets.

We invite you to embark on a travel experience unlike any other. Join us as we explore exotic lands and distinctive destinations, in exquisite style and comfort. At Hiqba Tourism, it’s our pleasure to make your next journey extraordinary.

Our Mission

Serving our customers by granting them incredible travel experiences, with the tiniest details flawlessly managed, so their vacations become lifetime memories and the world fits within the palm of their hands. We aim to offer ‘experiences’ for all people who love to travel.

Our Vision

To be the leading, most trusted travel & tourism management company in UAE in 5 years, and one of the most renowned leaders in the Tourism industry by 2030 known for unparalleled customer service, and offering affordable travel packages & life-changing travel experiences.

Our Values

Hiqba Tourism operates with a strict adherence to our foundational principles derived from our 5 core values - Honesty, Integrity, Transparency, Affordability & Reliability. These values drive us to bring the world closer to people, so no place on the globe is too distant and no adventure is out of reach.
